Travel Tips

💡 Click any card for more details

🌡️
Weather in April
Hot and humid, 28–35°C. Your dates (Apr 7–11) are ideal — fewer heavy showers. Schedule outdoor activities in the morning.
📱
Apps to Download
Grab — taxis & bikes (MUST HAVE)
Google Maps — navigation
Google Translate — scan Vietnamese text
Klook — tour bookings
💵
Money Tips
Withdraw VND at ATMs for best rates. Carry small bills (10k, 20k, 50k notes). Budget ~500k–700k VND/day per person for meals + transport.
🛺
Getting Around
Grab for most rides — always cheaper than street taxis. Metro Line 1 for Suoi Tien. Walking for District 1 — hotel is central.
🕌
Muslim Travel Tips
Always ask "Ada halal?" when unsure. Bring halal snacks for parks and tours. Prayer facilities may be limited — use Google Maps for nearby mosques.
🤝
Culture & Safety
Remove shoes at temples. Bargain at markets — start at 50% of asking price. Watch for pickpockets in crowds. Cross roads carefully — traffic is WILD.

Apartment Cafe Tip
The Cafe Apartment building has great views and multiple cafes inside. Take the stairs — there's a charge for the lift! Views from higher floors are worth the climb.
