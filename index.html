Suoi Tien — Full Day Theme Park!
🎢 Thrill Rides🌊 Water Park🐊 Crocodiles🎡 Adventure
7:30 AM
☕ Quick Breakfast Before Big Day HALAL ✅
Light breakfast at Katinat near hotel. Don't eat too heavy — you'll be on thrill rides soon! Grab a Ca Phe Sua Da to fuel up.

🚕 Grab to Suoi Tien TRAVEL
Book a Grab to 120 Xa Lộ Hà Nội, Thu Duc City — about 40-50 min from District 1 hotel. Arrive when gates open at 8:00 AM to beat the crowds on the dry rides!
~RM15-20 per car (split 4 ways)
🗺️ Maps
9:30 AM
🎟️ Collect Wristbands — Adventure Combo + Water Park FUN
Show your Agoda QR code at the entrance to collect wristbands. Store bags in locker (bring change for lockers). Apply sunscreen before entering! Adventure Combo + Water Park — everything is included in your RM62.88 package.
RM62.88 per person — booked via Agoda ✅
📅 View Booking on Agoda
9:45 AM
🎢 Thrill Rides — Hit Them While Dry! FUN
Go for the dry rides first before getting soaked in the water park. Must-rides included in the Adventure Combo:
🚀 Steel Roller Coaster — 1,200m track, 30m high, 60 km/h — biggest thrill in the park!
🌀 Space Spinner (Vòng Xoay Vũ Trụ)
🛸 Disc-Shaped UFO (Flying Saucer)
🪂 Sky Conqueror / Pendulum Ride
🚢 Dragon Boat / Viking Boat
11:00 AM
🔥 18 Levels of Hell — MUST DO! FUN
The most unique ride in Vietnam — a dark journey through Buddhist underworld scenes. Absolutely one-of-a-kind, you won't find this anywhere else! Part of your Adventure Combo — don't skip this!
11:30 AM
🐊 Crocodile Kingdom FUN
Witness and feed over 25,000 crocodiles — one of the largest crocodile reserves in the world. Absolutely wild and totally unique to Suoi Tien. Don't miss this iconic experience!
12:15 PM
🍽️ Lunch at Park Food Court
⚠️ Halal options are limited at Suoi Tien. Safest bets: fried rice, noodles, vegetarian dishes, fresh fruit. Bring your own halal snacks as backup! Fresh coconut water is widely available and affordable. Drinks ~RM4-6.
~RM10-18 per person
1:00 PM
🌊 Water Park — Tien Dong Ngoc Nu Beach FUN
Change into swimwear and hit the water park! Opens ~1:00 PM. Included in your Adventure Combo + Water Park package. Features: wave pool, 8 water slides, lazy river, dragon boat, water basketball, artificial beach. Keep valuables in locker! Spend a solid 2-3 hours here!
2:30 PM
🚂 Happy Train + Cultural Exploration FUN
Hop on the Happy Train for a scenic ride around the park — great way to dry off and see the cultural landmarks. Explore the golden dragon sculptures, Buddhist temples, and folklore exhibits. Take plenty of photos!
4:00 PM
🚪 Exit Park & Head Back to Hotel TRAVEL
Collect locker items, get changed and exit by 4:00–4:30 PM (park closes 5:00 PM). Book a Grab back to hotel — ~40-50 min. Shower and rest before dinner!
~RM15-20 per car back to hotel
6:30 PM
🍽️ Dinner — Pho Rahim or The Daun HALAL ✅
Well-deserved proper halal dinner after the massive day out! Pho Rahim for something light and comforting, or The Daun Restaurant for a fuller sit-down meal. Then rest — you'll need it!
~RM10-23 per person 🟢 Halal Verified
🗺️ Pho Rahim 🗺️ The Daun

🌡️
Weather in April
Hot and humid, 28–35°C. Schedule outdoor activities in the morning. Afternoon thunderstorms common.
📱
Apps to Download
Grab — taxis & bikes (MUST HAVE)
Google Maps — navigation
Google Translate — scan Vietnamese text
Agoda — River Cruise booking
💵
Money Tips
Withdraw VND at ATMs for best rates. Carry small bills. Budget ~500k–700k VND/day per person for meals + transport.
🛺
Getting Around
Grab for most rides — always cheaper than street taxis. Walking for District 1 — hotel is very central. Out-of-area cafes need a Grab.
🕌
Muslim Travel Tips
Always ask "Ada halal?" when unsure. Bring halal snacks for non-halal venues. Dress modestly at religious sites. Smart-casual for Landmark 81 and River Cruise.
📸
Photo Tips
Oasis Cafe: Go after 3 PM. Teddy Bear Cafe: Weekday mornings. Blank Sky: Arrive 5 PM sharp. Pink Church: 8-10 AM best light.
🤝
Culture & Safety
Remove shoes at temples. Bargain at markets — start at 40-50% of asking price. Watch for pickpockets. Cross roads carefully — traffic is WILD.
